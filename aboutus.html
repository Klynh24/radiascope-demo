RadiaScope

RadiaScope is an advanced software solution utilizing image recognition technology to transform CT scan data into highly detailed 3D models. The platform is designed to classify pulmonary anatomical components, enabling physicians to quickly and accurately detect and evaluate abnormalities. RadiaScope also supports longitudinal disease monitoring, offering alerts and labels for atypical components to aid doctors in deciding on necessary interventions, from biopsies to pharmaceutical treatments.

Our Vision

RadiaScope envisions revolutionizing lung disease diagnosis and treatment through advanced imaging technology. We aim to empower physicians with robust tools to make more precise clinical decisions, enhance patient outcomes, and elevate global healthcare quality.

Our Values

We deliver high value to our users through optimal diagnostic accuracy, intuitive design, decision support, disease tracking, and global collaboration for shared expertise.
